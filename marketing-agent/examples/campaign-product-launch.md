## Content Assets Checklist

### Launch Day (Week 1)

- [ ] Product Hunt listing (title, tagline, description, images)
- [ ] Launch blog post (2000 words, SEO-optimized)
- [ ] LinkedIn announcement post + 5-tweet thread
- [ ] Email to existing customers (upsell angle)
- [ ] Email to leads (new feature announcement)
- [ ] Press release for PR outreach

### Week 1-2

- [ ] 3x LinkedIn thought leadership posts
- [ ] Demo video (2 min product tour)
- [ ] Customer testimonial video (3 beta users)
- [ ] Webinar slide deck + script
- [ ] Follow-up email sequence (3 emails)

### Ongoing (Week 3-8)

- [ ] 2x blog posts per week (use cases, tutorials)
- [ ] 5x LinkedIn posts per week
- [ ] Weekly email to nurture list
- [ ] Paid ad creative refresh at Week 4
